# ⚖️ Module 1: AI & Law - Lund University

This module explores the fundamental interaction between Artificial Intelligence systems and legal frameworks. It focuses on the shift from reactive legislation to proactive ex-ante governance, addressing the "Foreseeability Gap" and liability challenges.

---

## 📚 Primary Source Material
The analysis in this module is based on the foundational research paper from the **Harvard/SSRN** database:
* **Research Paper:** [Regulating Artificial Intelligence Systems: Risks, Challenges, Competencies, and Strategies](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=2609777)
* **Author:** Matthew U. Scherer (Harvard Journal of Law & Technology)
