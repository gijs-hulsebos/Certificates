# 🎯 Module 1: AI Applications in Marketing & Finance - Wharton Business School
### *AI and the Customer Journey: From Segmentation to Individualization*

This module analyzes how AI transforms the traditional marketing funnel into a dynamic, individualized experience. As an AI Automation Engineer, I focus on the technical implementation of **Recommendation Engines**, the prediction of **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)**, and the automation of hyper-personalized engagement at scale.

### 🛠️ Research Methodology: AI-Augmented Strategic Synthesis
All analyses in this module are synthesized using a private **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** environment to bridge marketing psychology with technical automation.

* **Environment:** Developed a high-context knowledge base in **NotebookLM**, indexing Wharton transcripts, papers on recommendation algorithms, and predictive marketing frameworks.
* **Process:** This private "Digital Twin" of the curriculum is used to evaluate how **agentic workflows (n8n)** can automate personalized customer interactions and optimize the firm's conversion rates through algorithmic judgment.
* **IP Policy:** To respect Wharton's academic copyrights, the raw research environment remains private. Derived strategic assets and intelligence reports are available in this repository.
