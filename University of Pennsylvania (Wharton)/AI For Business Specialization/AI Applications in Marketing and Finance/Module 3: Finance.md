### 🛠️ Research Methodology: AI-Augmented Strategic Synthesis
All analyses in this module are synthesized using a private **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** environment to bridge financial theory with technical automation.

* **Environment:** Developed a high-context knowledge base in **NotebookLM**, indexing Wharton transcripts, papers on algorithmic risk, and fintech innovation reports.
* **Process:** This private "Digital Twin" of the curriculum is used to evaluate how **agentic workflows (n8n)** can be applied to financial data processing, compliance monitoring, and automated decision-making in high-stakes environments.
* **IP Policy:** To respect Wharton's academic copyrights, the raw research environment remains private. Derived strategic assets and intelligence reports are available in this repository.
