### *Personalisation: Hyper-Individualization & Algorithmic Loyalty*

This module deep-dives into the evolution of **Hyper-Personalization**. We move beyond static segmentation into the world of real-time, 1-to-1 interactions. ### 🛠️ Research Methodology: AI-Augmented Strategic Synthesis
All analyses in this module are synthesized using a private **RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)** environment to bridge customer data science with technical automation.

* **Environment:** Developed a high-context knowledge base in **NotebookLM**, indexing Wharton transcripts, academic research on bandit algorithms, and case studies on real-time personalization leaders (e.g., Netflix, Amazon).
* **Process:** This private "Digital Twin" of the curriculum is used to evaluate how **agentic workflows (n8n)** can move from "one-size-fits-all" automation to adaptive systems that learn from individual user behavior in real-time.
* **IP Policy:** To respect Wharton's academic copyrights, the raw research environment remains private. Derived strategic assets and intelligence reports are available in this repository.
